# Hotel Booking Analysis

## Overview
This project analyzes hotel booking data to extract actionable business insights related to booking behavior, cancellations, revenue trends, and customer preferences.

The objective is to support data-driven decision-making for improving occupancy rates, reducing cancellations, and maximizing profitability.

---

- Data Visualization (Matplotlib, Seaborn)

## Business Recommendations

### 1. Strategies to Reduce Cancellations
- Implement stricter cancellation policies during high-risk periods (July, August, December), including partial refunds or time-based penalties.
- Introduce incentives such as discounts for non-refundable or early-payment bookings.
- Target high-cancellation segments (Standard rooms, Travel Agents) with better pricing transparency and commitment-based offers.
- Optimize lead time management through reminders and retention-focused benefits.

---

### 2. Improve Profitability & Increase Repeat Bookings
- Focus on mid-range segments (3–4 star, Deluxe rooms) that offer optimal balance between demand and margins.
- Promote direct booking channels (Web/App) to reduce dependency on high-commission intermediaries.
- Introduce loyalty programs, cashback offers, and personalized recommendations.
- Use data-driven strategies to upsell customers from Standard to Deluxe rooms.

---

### 3. Optimize Pricing, Promotions & Channel Strategy
- Implement dynamic pricing based on demand patterns and seasonality.
- Offer targeted promotions during high-cancellation periods.
- Adjust pricing strategies for price-sensitive segments to reduce cancellations.
- Strengthen Web/App platforms with better user experience and exclusive deals.
- Reduce reliance on Travel Agents by optimizing commission structures.

---

## Conclusion
The analysis highlights key behavioral and operational patterns that directly impact hotel performance. By leveraging these insights, businesses can enhance customer retention, optimize pricing strategies, and improve overall revenue efficiency.
